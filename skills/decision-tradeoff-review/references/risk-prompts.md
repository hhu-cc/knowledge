# Risk Prompts for Tradeoff Review

Use these prompts to pressure-test a recommendation.

## Stage fit

- Is this option right for the current stage, or only right in a more mature future state?
- What are we optimizing too early?

## Downside risk

- If this option fails, what is the real cost?
- Who bears that cost?
- Is the downside reversible?

## Opportunity cost

- What are we not doing if we choose this?
- Which better option becomes harder later because of this decision?

## Capability match

- Does the current team actually have the skills, time, and attention to execute this well?
- Is the recommendation correct in theory but fragile in practice?

## Hidden burden

- What maintenance, coordination, or review overhead appears after implementation?
- Does this option create new dependencies or approval bottlenecks?

## Assumptions

- Which assumptions must be true for this to work?
- Which assumption is the weakest?
- What small test could validate it before full commitment?
